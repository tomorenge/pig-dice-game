PIG DICE GAME

Rules of the game

This is a game of chance that involves two players each waiting for their turn to roll a dice with the aim of scoring 100 points to emerge the winner

rules

  • A player rolls the dice when its their turn to play
  • if the dice land and the player gets 1 their total score translates to 0 and it becomes the next player's turn
  • if the player rolls between 2-6 the score is added to their cummulativescore and they can play again or hold
  • Roll on untill either player score 100 points to win the game
